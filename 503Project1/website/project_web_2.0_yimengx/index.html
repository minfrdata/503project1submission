Background

Driving is one of the most common ways to communicate in United States. Almost every family will get involved with buying or selling vehicle. Auto sales in US market never disappointed manufacturers. Strong economic condition brings higher demands for the cars market. In 2017, US has 39.3 million used-car sales, increasing 1.8% from 2016. 2018 has an even strong economic figure, in which higher sales are expected.

In this project, we used analytical data visualization tools to explore the US used car market. First, we conduct data collection & cleaning. Then we analyze the price and volume pattern of used cars market. After having an overall understanding of sales and pricing trend, we combine the economic, population, and public transportation data to find the potential reason for the different sales amount in each state. Finally, to have a closer look at cars of different style and price level , we select several tyical models to compare the sales and price.
