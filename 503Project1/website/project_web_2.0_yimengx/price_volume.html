Section Summary Sales Volume and Price of Used Cars

Used cars market are expanding these years. It will be very important to understand the sales volumes and price trend of the used car market. In this part, we want to combine various features to visually explore the data. With the help of effective visualization, we are able to get a comprehensive view of automobile sales from different perspectives.

This page gives the solution for the subquestions:

  • What is the volume distribution by car features, such as brands, certificated, bodystyle, registeration year?
  • Who are the main participants of the used cars market?
  • What is the price trend for cars in different generation

sumary of results.

Heat Map Popular Brands

There are hundreds of manufacturers in the market. A straightforward question is who are dominating the market. First of all, we choose the top sellers from US market and use heatmap to compare the sales of these top sellers brands.

Generally, American brands are have biggest share. Top 2 are both American brands, while the following 3 Japanese brands almost have the same sales volume as the top 2. Some luxury brands such as BMW and Mercedes-Benz are also very popular.

SunBurst Car Features

Apart from brands, we also segment used cars market by car features, including whether certificated or not, which body styles, which years these cars were produced. The sunburst graph gives a clear view of hierarchical structure of the cars in the market.

From the inner circle, over 90% of the cars are certificated. Then under certificated, the distribution of body style tells that SUV is the most popular style and Sedan come as the second one. American customer seem prefer the bigger cars. The outer circle gives some idea which years were most cars registered. The majority of used cars has been produced in the recent 5 years. p>
